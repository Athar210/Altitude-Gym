ABOUT US




ALTITUDE GYM is a way of life and not just a gym. Founded in 2011 by Sheema Sultan, the current branch in OceanTower, Clifton, is our first multi-function full-service facility in Pakistan. Spread out over one 15,000 square floor, 14 stories high up in the Ocean Tower office building, Clifton. The service model is designed for all levels of fitness and lifestyles. A wide variety of training and studio services that have been researched and qualified from all over the world with proven results.
